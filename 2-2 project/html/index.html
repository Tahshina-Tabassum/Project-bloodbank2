About Our Blood Bank Website

Blood is a life saving resource that lies within all of us. The need for blood donations is constant and it is our responsibility to ensure that there is a healthy and reliable supply in the Cayman Islands through empowerment and education. We believe in empowering our local community by educating them about the importance of blood donations and the simplicity around the process of donating.
